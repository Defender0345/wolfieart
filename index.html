VISIT STUDIO

Fine artist | Illustrator | Photographer

Hello my name is Ankia, I’m an asspiring young artist located in the western cape, south africa. i draw inspiration from my surroundings and the beautiful nature around me. The name wolfie art comes from my long standing relationship with wolves and how my interactions with them has shaped part of who i am. my art is processed based working with themes and concepts of questioning, mythology, heritage, understanding and interpretation or misinterpretation. Most of my works contain imigarey of wolves and crows as viewed from my perspective. Through my commision works i find joy in making my clients visions a reality through my art.
